Beta Ji charges a flat rate per bag, so heavier bags cost the same as light ones. That's a huge win for students shipping textbooks, winter clothes, and home goods.

Luggage Size Guide

Type of Luggage Dimensions (inches) Dimensions (cm) Capacity (liters) Uses
Personal Items 18 × 14 × 8 46 × 36 × 20 10–20 L Essentials, electronics, snacks
Carry-on / Small Checked 22 × 14 × 9 56 × 36 × 23 35–45 L Short trips (2–3 days) or weekend getaways
Medium Checked Bag 24 × 16 × 10 61 × 41 × 25 60–80 L Week-long trips (4–5 days)
Large Checked Bag 28 × 18 × 12 71 × 46 × 30 90–100 L Long travels (7+ days)
Extra Large Checked Bag 32 × 20 × 14 81 × 51 × 36 120–140 L Extended trips, family vacations, relocations

💡 Tip for Beta Ji bookings: Most airlines limit checked bags to 158 cm total dimensions (L+W+H). If your bag is within these limits, Beta Ji will handle it. Oversized bags attract special handling fees of ₹2,500–4,000 on airlines — we ship them for the same flat rate!
