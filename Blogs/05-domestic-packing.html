Domestic Travel Packing Guide: 2-Day, 5-Day, and 10-Day Trips

📅 Published: January 15, 2025 ⏱️ 7 minute read 👤 By Beta Ji Travel Team

Packing for a domestic flight in India doesn't have to be stressful. Whether you're taking a quick 2-day trip to see family, a week-long vacation, or a 10-day work assignment, there's a science to packing efficiently. Pack too much and you'll pay excess baggage fees. Pack too little and you'll be buying things you forgot.

This guide breaks down exactly what to pack for different trip lengths.

The Golden Rule: One Outfit Per Day, Plus Two Extras

The secret to packing light is this: one outfit per day + one casual outfit + one going-out outfit = your entire wardrobe.

That's it. Everything else is backup.

The 2-Day Trip: Carry-On Only

Trip type: Quick weekend getaway, family visit, business meeting

Luggage Size:

  • Carry-on roller bag (22" × 14" × 9")
  • Personal item (7 kg max)
  • No checked baggage needed

What to Pack:

  • 2 tops (1 casual, 1 semi-formal)
  • 1 pair comfortable pants
  • 1 pair casual shoes + 1 formal shoe
  • Underwear for 3 days
  • Sleepwear
  • Jacket or sweater
  • Toiletries (travel-sized)
  • Phone charger
  • Medications (if any)
  • Important documents (ID, ticket)

Skip:

  • ❌ Heavy jeans
  • ❌ Multiple shoe pairs
  • ❌ Full-size bottles
  • ❌ Multiple accessories
💡 Pro Tip: Wear your bulkiest outfit (jacket, heavy shoes) during travel. This saves space in your bag and keeps you warm on the flight.

The 5-Day Trip: Small Checked Bag

Trip type: Week-long family visit, short vacation, work trip

Luggage Size:

  • Carry-on (22" × 14" × 9") +
  • Small checked bag (24" × 16" × 10")
  • Total: ~40 kg of luggage

What to Pack:

  • 5 tops (mix of casual and semi-formal)
  • 2 pairs of pants/skirts
  • 1 dress or blazer (if needed for events)
  • 2 pairs of shoes (casual + formal)
  • Jacket/sweater
  • Underwear for 6 days
  • Sleepwear + slippers
  • Full toiletries kit
  • Phone charger + laptop charger (if needed)
  • Medications
  • Important documents
  • 1 book or entertainment

The 10-Day Trip: Standard Checked Bag

Trip type: Extended family stay, long vacation, relocation for work

Luggage Size:

  • Carry-on (22" × 14" × 9") +
  • Standard checked bag (28" × 18" × 12")
  • Total: ~50 kg of luggage

What to Pack:

  • 7-8 tops (variety of casual, semi-formal, formal)
  • 3 pairs of bottoms
  • 2 dresses or blazers
  • 3 pairs of shoes (casual, formal, athletic)
  • Light jacket + warm jacket
  • Underwear for 11 days
  • Sleepwear + slippers
  • Socks and accessories
  • Complete toiletries kit + medications
  • Phone + laptop + other chargers
  • Important documents
  • Books, entertainment, tech accessories

Universal Packing Tips (All Trip Lengths)

1. Choose Neutral Colors

Pack black, navy, white, khaki, and gray. Everything matches everything. You'll have more outfit combinations with fewer items.

2. Roll Clothes, Don't Fold

Rolling takes 30% less space than folding. Your clothes also wrinkle less.

3. Use Packing Cubes

Organize by type: one cube for tops, one for bottoms, one for underwear. Easy to find things and saves space.

4. Wear Your Bulkiest Items

Wear your jacket, heaviest shoes, and warmest clothes during travel. Don't pack them.

5. Pack Toiletries Last

Put toiletries on top so you can access them easily at the airport or hotel.

6. Bring a Scarf

One scarf can be a shawl, beach cover-up, pillow, or extra layer. Super versatile.

Final Tips by Season

Summer Travel (March–May)

  • Light, breathable fabrics
  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
  • Hat or cap
  • Sunglasses
  • Light layers (for AC indoors)

Monsoon Travel (June–August)

  • Waterproof jacket or umbrella
  • Quick-dry fabrics
  • Closed-toe shoes (water-resistant)
  • Extra socks
  • Plastic bags (for wet clothes)

Winter Travel (November–February)

  • Warm jacket or sweater
  • Layers (thermal innerwear)
  • Warm socks
  • Scarf or shawl
  • Closed-toe shoes
